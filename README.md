## 🌍 English

### Why generic side-hustle advice fails

You've seen the same lists a thousand times:
*"Start a dropshipping store. Become a freelance writer. Sell on Amazon FBA."*

Those lists fail for one reason: **they were written before the author knew anything about you**. So a stay-at-home mom, a senior HR director, a CS student, and an overseas worker all get the same 10 suggestions. Of course nothing fits.

### What this does instead

You give it three things:
- **Background** — what you've actually done
- **Skills** — what you're actually good at
- **Situation** — what time, money, and energy you actually have

It returns:
- 🎯 **3 monetization paths** designed for your specific combination
- 💰 **Why each path fits you** (not someone else)
- 🚀 **The exact first move** — what to make this week to earn your first $100

> The point isn't to give you "more options." It's to give you **paths shaped to your shoes.**

## 🧠 The Idea Behind This · 核心理念

> **This is not a tool. It's a decision system.**
> **这不是一个工具，是一套决策系统。**

Instead of asking AI for an answer, you use AI to:
不是问 AI 要答案，而是用 AI 走完这条链：

```
Define · 定义 → Analyze · 分析 → Design · 设计 → Execute · 执行
```

The same philosophy runs through all my projects: AI works best when you treat it as a **system you design**, not a chatbot you query.
所有项目共同的哲学：AI 用得好的关键，是把它当**你设计的系统**，不是当你提问的聊天机器人。
